A small sanctuary of green

On the edge of the jungle in Wanchawala, The Jungle Loft feels calm and close to nature — with palms, frangipani and birdsong around a quiet pool. From here, beaches and Galle Fort are just a short ride away.

Our four stays — Peacock Villa, Monkey Villa, Tropical Mango Bungalow and Golden Lotus Bungalow — are crafted in a warm, natural palette with hand‑picked antiques, local wood and soft linens. Morning light filters through the leaves, evenings are for tea on the terrace.

Design & Soul

We like beautiful simplicity: tactile fabrics, reclaimed details, quiet color. Things are made to last and to feel good — not to shout.
